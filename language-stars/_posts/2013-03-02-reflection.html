It's been about 2 years of Leilani at language stars, so it's a little past time to reflect on what she's learned and how it's helped her Spanish in our bi-lingual family.  

For those of you that don't want to read my entire blog, jerks, I'll summarize.  For about two years, maybe longer, we've been taking my daughter to Language Stars to supplement her Spanish learning.  When I say supplement it's because my wife is from Peru and speaks to her in Spanish at home.  As hard as they work at Language Stars there's no doubt in my mind her home learning is far more effective than anything they can do.
